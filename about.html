The Project

What We're Building & Why

Our project explores the architecture, deployment, and scalability of cloud-based solutions as part of our Cloud Computing course. We investigate how organisations can leverage platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud to build resilient, cost-efficient, and highly available systems.

Through hands-on experimentation and research, we examine key cloud concepts including virtualisation, containerisation, serverless computing, and cloud security — translating classroom theory into practical, real-world insights.

Team ALPHA brings together skills in development, design, and version control to deliver a project that is both technically sound and thoughtfully presented.
